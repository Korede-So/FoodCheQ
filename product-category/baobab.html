Product Description

Baobab powder is made from the fruit of the baobab tree (Adansonia digitata), native to Africa. This nutrient-dense superfood is rich in vitamin C, fiber, antioxidants, and essential minerals. Our finely ground baobab powder has a tangy, citrus-like flavor that makes it a versatile addition to smoothies, yogurt, oatmeal, and baked goods. Each 200g package provides a convenient way to incorporate the health benefits of baobab into your daily diet.

Health Benefits

  • Rich source of vitamin C — supports immune and skin health
  • High in soluble and insoluble fiber — supports healthy digestion and regularity
  • Contains antioxidants and polyphenols — helps neutralize free radicals
  • Good source of minerals (potassium, calcium, magnesium) — supports electrolyte balance
  • Prebiotic properties — may promote a healthy gut microbiome
