Product Description

Passion Flower Tea is a calming herbal infusion made from the dried leaves and flowering parts of the Passiflora vine. Traditionally used in herbal medicine for centuries, passion flower has a gentle, floral flavour and a soothing aroma. This premium quality tea is carefully processed to preserve its natural alkaloids and active compounds including flavonoids and passiflorine. Each cup delivers a warm embrace of herbal comfort, making it an ideal evening ritual for those seeking gentle relaxation and restful sleep support without caffeine.
