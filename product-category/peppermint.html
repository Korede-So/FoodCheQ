Product Description

Peppermint Tea is a refreshing herbal infusion made from the dried leaves of Mentha piperita, a hybrid mint plant prized for its cooling, invigorating flavour and aroma. This premium quality peppermint tea delivers the classic bright, minty taste that soothes digestion and uplifts the senses. Naturally caffeine-free and packed with beneficial volatile oils including menthol, peppermint is a beloved classic in herbal wellness traditions worldwide. Each cup provides a perfect post-meal comfort or afternoon refreshment without the stimulation of caffeine.
