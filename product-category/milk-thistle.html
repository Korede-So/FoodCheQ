Product Description

Milk Thistle is a powerful liver-supporting herb derived from the seeds of Silybum marianum, traditionally valued for promoting hepatic health. Our premium quality milk thistle seeds are carefully processed to preserve their silymarin content, the herb's most bioactive compound. This spiky purple flowering plant has been used for over 2,000 years to support liver function and detoxification. Each 100g package provides a concentrated botanical source. Perfect for herbal teas, extracts, or traditional wellness protocols.

  • Rich in silymarin for hepatic support

  • Supports healthy bile production
  • Rich in antioxidants that protect against free radicals
